Public beta

GridPool

Decentralized reward sharing for sovereign Bitcoin miners. GridPool lets miners keep their own block templates while sharing high-difficulty proofs to reduce solo-style payout variance.

  • DATUM support today
  • Hydrapool path in progress
  • No centralized payout wallet
